# About Me

Hi! I am Agni Datta, a final-year graduate student pursuing an integrated Master of Technology in Computer Science with a specialization in Cybersecurity at VIT Bhopal University, Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh, India. My academic focus encompasses cybersecurity, digital forensics, and digital resilience, with a particular emphasis on proof systems and provable security. My research primarily concentrates on theoretical computer science and cryptography.

## Research Interests

My research interests primarily focus on cryptography, with specific emphasis on the following areas:

- Zero-knowledge proofs
- Rational proofs
- Provable security  

It is important to note that this list is not exhaustive. I maintain an open stance towards exploring any topic related to the practical application of computational complexity and cryptographic primitives.
