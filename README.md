# About Me

My name is Agni Datta. I am a final-year graduate student pursuing an integrated M.Tech in Computer Science with a minor in Cybersecurity at VIT Bhopal University, Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh, India. I have a formal background in cybersecurity and digital resilience. I am particularly interested in proof systems and provable security, and my research is primarily focused on theoretical computer science and cryptography.

## Research Interests

Broadly speaking, the following comprise the core of my research interests:

```
Zero-knowledge proofs;
Rational proofs;
Secure multiparty computation;
Provable security.
```

Please keep in mind that this list is by no means exhaustive. I am open to exploring any topic related to the practical use of computational complexity and cryptographic primitives.
